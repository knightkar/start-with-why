# Start With Why – Visual Single-Page Summary

A minimalist, Apple-style, animated single-page summary of  
**_Start With Why_ by Simon Sinek**.

This repo is designed as:

- A **GitHub Pages**-ready site
- A **visual, scroll-based explanation** of the book’s core ideas
- A **starting template** for future visual book summaries

## Concepts Covered

This page summarizes the major ideas of _Start With Why_:

- The **Golden Circle**: WHY → HOW → WHAT
- The **power of WHY** in leadership and organizations
- The **biology of decision-making** (limbic vs neocortex)
- **Trust, authenticity, and consistency**
- The **Law of Diffusion of Innovation** and tipping points
- How to **apply WHY** to your life and work

All text is an original summary, not copied from the book.
