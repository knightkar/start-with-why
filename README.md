## 📘 Concepts Covered

This page summarizes the major ideas of **Start With Why**, including:

- The **Golden Circle**: WHY → HOW → WHAT  
- The power of **WHY** in leadership and organizations  
- The **biology of decision-making** (limbic vs. neocortex)  
- **Trust, authenticity, and consistency**  
- The **Law of Diffusion of Innovation**  
- How to **apply WHY** to your life and work  
- Additional concepts:
  - Manipulations vs. Inspiration  
  - The Celery Test  
  - Split Happens (losing the WHY)  
  - Real-world examples (Apple, Southwest, Harley-Davidson, MLK, Wright Brothers)

All text is an **original summary**, not copied from the book.
